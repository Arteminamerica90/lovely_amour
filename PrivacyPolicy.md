# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 6, 2025

## 1. Introduction

The “Lovely Words” application (“App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Favorite phrases:** The App stores the phrases you add to favorites locally on your device.
- **App settings:** Your preferences and settings are stored locally.

### 2.2 Information We Do Not Collect
- We **DO NOT** collect personal data (name, email, phone number).
- We **DO NOT** collect location data.
- We **DO NOT** collect usage analytics.
- We **DO NOT** use tracking or advertising identifiers.
- We **DO NOT** collect payment information (all purchases are processed by the App Store).

## 3. How We Use Information

All information is stored **locally on your device** and used solely to:
- Save your favorite phrases
- Save your in-app preferences
- Improve the experience within the App

## 4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms (UserDefaults, Core Data). We **do not share** your data with third parties and we **do not store** it on external servers.

## 5. Premium Subscription

If you purchase a Premium subscription:
- Payments are processed through the App Store
- We do not have access to your payment details
- Subscription status is stored locally on your device
- We do not share subscription information with third parties

## 6. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all app data at any time by removing the App from your device
- Cancel the subscription via App Store settings
- Avoid using features that require data to be saved

## 7. Security

We rely on standard iOS security mechanisms to protect your data:
- On-device data encryption
- Safeguards provided by the iOS security framework
- No network transfer of your data

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through an App update.
